NANITAL

Nainital, often referred to as the "Lake District of India," is a picturesque hill station nestled in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, situated at an elevation of about 2,084 meters.

DEHRADUN

Dehradun is a captivating city with picturesque landscapes and serves as a gateway to Uttarakhand's natural wonders.As the capital of the northern Indian state of Uttarakhand, Dehradun serves as a gateway to some of the most breathtaking natural wonders in the country

ALMORA

Almora, a picturesque hill town nestled in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and serene ambiance. Situated at an altitude of around 1,638 meters (5,374 feet) above sea level, Almora is a popular destination for those seeking an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

CHAMOLI

Chamoli is the birthplace of the Chipko Movement and attracts nature lovers as much as it beckons the religiously bent. History has it that Chamoli was carved out of the former Garhwal district in 1960.When in Chamoli, it is imperative to try local and traditional cuisine. Predominantly grain and cereal-based, incorporate fresh local produce.

THERIGARHWAL

Tehri Garhwal is on of the sacred hilly districts of Uttarakhand State. Before the creation of universe, Lord Brahma is said to have meditated on this sacred land. Muni-ki-Reti and Tapovan of the district are the places of penance for the ancient Rishis. Its hilly terrain and lack of easy communications have helped it to preserve its culture almost intact.

PITHORAGARH

Pithoragarh has great strategic significance and is also a politically sensitive district along the northern frontier of India.Having tremendous strategic importance as the passes of Lipulekh, Kungribingri, Pithoragarh is the last district adjoining Tibet. Till 2019, the Ministry of External Affairs conducted the Kailash Mansarovar Yatra via Pithoragarh District, which was done by trek.
