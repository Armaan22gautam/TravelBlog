SHIMLA

The Queen of Hills

The Capital Town of Himachal Pradesh. It was formerly the summer capital during the British Rule.

Nainital

The Lake District

The charming Himalayan lake town, is a picture-postcard perfect hill-station and one of the most popular in Northern India.

BHIMTAL

A Picturesque Town

Named after the legendary mythological character Bhima, this town is famous for its serene Bhimtal Lake.

CHAIL

A summer retreat

Frequented by hikers as it offers wonderful views of the lower Himalayas. It has good trekking points present all over.

RISHIKESH

Place of Sagas

The hills of the morning sun, the river of flowing water , mountain peaks covered with fog is the experience felt here.

LUNGTA

The Camping Estate

The hidden gem in Dharmshala known for its camping sites and waterfall attracts the nature enthusiasts

DALHOUISE

Creator of Contemporary India

KEDARNATH

The Lord Of Field

The town is home to the Kedarnath Temple, which is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva

MCLODGANJ

The Little Lhasa

Mcleodganj is famous for its spiritual ambiance, Tibetan culture and as the seat of the Tibetan government-in-exile.

MANALI

Valley of Gods

Manali is a hot spot destination for tourists who like to enjoy adventure activities and explore the scenic nature.

KASAULI

Land of Unmisted Rejuvenating Breeze

Kasauli famous for its Sunset Point, Christ Church and Mall Road.

SPITI VALLEY

Land of Adventure

Spiti is a religious and cultural center for Buddhists, and that is another reason that makes it such a famous tourist attraction.

KASOL

Mini Israel

Kasol serves as the gateway to some of Himachal Pradesh's most scenic treks, including the famous Kheerganga trek.

KUFRI

Snow Queen

Kufri, the winter sports capital of Shimla, leaves each and every visitor splendid with its beautiful white cover of snow.

UTTARKASHI

Devbhumi

Uttarakhand truly earned its nickname, "the land of the gods" (Devbhumi).

ROHTANG

Gateway of Lahol and Spiti

Known for its scenic beauty, Rohtang Pass holds strategic importance for India.
